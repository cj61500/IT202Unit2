Argen


URL

https://arngren.net/

This page is unappealing because..

  1. From what seems to be a Norwegian Ebay almost, there is no flow to the site at all.
  2. There are two different menu bars that look to serve the same purpose, on separate parts of the screen.
  3. The contact details for some reason are in the middle of the page, in between all the listings.

Recommendations for improvement

  1. Definitely remove the fact that the page scrolls to both the right and down. This would help solve the organization.
  2. Now that the page ONLY scrolls down, we can move the second menu, to below the first, making one continuous menu.
  3. Adjust the way the products are displayed. Only show a certain amount at once and show only similarly related products in groups.

I would definitely not recommend this site as 1 I'm sure you can find any of this on Amazon, and 2 I'm not too sure they ship internationally!
